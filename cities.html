Top 3 cities I've called home

  • Denver
  • Boulder
  • Boston
  • Arezzo, Italy
  • Hamburg, Germany
  • Sandy, Oregon

I was born and raised in Denver, and have lived there on and off my whole life. I moved to Boston to attend Conservatory for Acting for four years, between 2008-2012. My junior year of college, I studied Commedia Dell'Arte and Physical Theatre, in Arezzo, Italy. Afterward, I backpacked through Europe and spent a month in Hamburg, Germany working on a farm. I moved back to Boulder after graduation and have been there on and off ever since. In between living in Boulder, I moved back to Denver for a time, and also moved just outside of Portland, Oregon in a small town near Mt. Hood for about 6 months. Currently, I'm living in Boulder after having lived in a trailer for 6 months mainly in California and the Southwest.
